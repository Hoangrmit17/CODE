The Secret
Sea ★
With foam that sweeps the shoreline wide
Its waves may crash, its winds may call,
Yet secrets lie beneath all.
Dive past the light where silence grows, Where mystery in darkness flows
Beneath the chum, a calmer place,
A hidden, deep, enchanted space.
This world beneath the sunlit sea
Breathes life in quiet mystery.
Unseen by most, yet rich and wide
A second Earth, beneath the tide.
